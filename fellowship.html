Questions

Straight answers.

Is it remote?
Yes, fully. It is remote and flexible, so you work through the weekly material and deadlines on your own schedule.
Is it paid?
There is no fee to take part; the fellowship is free. It is a learning programme, not a paid position, so there is no stipend either.
Who can apply?
Students, recent graduates, and people switching careers. No prerequisites beyond the commitment to do the weekly work.
What’s the time commitment per week?
Flexible. It is self-paced within weekly deadlines, so plan for a few focused hours each week to keep up with the work and feedback.
When does the next cohort start?
Rolling; apply anytime. Seats are limited and we review applications as they arrive, so earlier is better.
